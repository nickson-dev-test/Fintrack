Privacy Policy for FinTrack

Last updated: April 24, 2025

Thank you for choosing FinTrack. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (if any), how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

  • Definition: Personal Data refers to information that can be used to identify you, such as your name or email address.
  • Collection: We do not collect, store, or use any Personal Data through FinTrack.
  • Exception: If you contact us at nickforgesolutions@gmail.com, we will use your email address solely to respond to your inquiry and will delete it thereafter.

1.2 User-Entered Data

  • Definition: Any data you manually enter into FinTrack (e.g., budgets, transactions).
  • Storage: All such data remains only on your device.
  • Use: We do not transmit, view, or otherwise process your user-entered data.

1.3 Device Data (Non-Personal Data)

  • Definition: Information that does not personally identify you (e.g., device model, OS version, country code).
  • Purpose: We may collect this Non-Personal Data to help us debug issues, understand device compatibility, and improve app performance.
  • Anonymity: This data is aggregated and cannot be tied back to any individual user.

2. Advertising

  • Ad Networks: FinTrack displays advertisements served by third-party ad networks.
  • Data Collected by Ad Networks: These networks may collect Non-Personal Data (e.g., device identifiers, usage data, clickstream, and location) via cookies or local storage to deliver targeted ads.
  • Liability: FinTrack disclaims responsibility for any data practices of third-party ad providers. Please refer to each ad network’s privacy policy for details.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

  • Subscription Plans: FinTrack offers premium features through subscription plans billed on a recurring basis.
  • Payment Processing: All subscription payments are handled securely via the Google Play billing system; we do not collect or store your payment information directly.
  • Auto-Renewal & Cancellation: Subscriptions automatically renew each period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current term. You can manage or cancel your subscription any time through your Google Play account settings.
  • Refunds: Refunds are subject to Google Play’s refund policy. Please contact Google Play support for refund requests.

4. How We Use Your Information

  1. Support & Communication: Only if you email us at nickforgesolutions@gmail.com, to provide support or answer questions you’ve raised.
  2. App Improvement: Aggregate Device Data and ad-network analytics help us identify bugs, plan new features, and optimize performance.
  3. Security: We employ standard security measures (e.g., local encryption at rest) to protect data stored on your device.

5. Data Retention

  • Personal & User-Entered Data: None retained by us; all user data resides on your device until deletion.
  • Device & Ad Data: Retained only as needed for analytics and debugging, then purged according to best practices.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Ad Networks: We integrate leading mobile ad platforms (e.g. AdMob). Their data collection practices are governed by their own policies.
  • Payment Processor: Google Play handles all billing; see Google’s privacy documentation for details.

7. Children’s Privacy

FinTrack is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from minors.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • Contact & Deletion: Email us at nickforgesolutions@gmail.com to request deletion of any stored contact information.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You may disable diagnostics/analytics in your device settings where supported.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. The “Last updated” date will reflect such changes.
